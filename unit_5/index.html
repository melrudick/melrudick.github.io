Cody Roth in Chaccohuallasco, Peru

The air is thin and crisp at 4,500 meters, and Cody is forced to rest after hammering in his second bolt of Cocodrilo. The high altitude crag situated in Pitumarca boasts drippy limestone tufas that are almost spongy, yet strangely solid. We ask Cody about what he describes as his most unique trip to date.
